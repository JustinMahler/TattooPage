Tattoo Aftercare Instructions



PLEASE BE AWARE THAT TOUCH-UPS ARE TO BE DONE NO EARLIER THAN 30 DAYS AFTER APPLICATION AND NO LATER THAN 90 DAYS



PLEASE FOLLOW THESE AFTERCARE INSTRUCTIONS:


DO’S


Careful remove the bandage after 1-2 hours and clean it immediately Repeat these steps in order 4-5 times daily for 7-10 days. Wash hands with soap and water
Gently wash the tattooed area with soap and Luke warm water
Gently rinse all soap residue off with cool water
Gently pat dry with a clean towel
Sparingly apply a very thin layer of our Recovery© brand aftercare or Aquaphor©
After the initial 7-10 day period stop using the Recovery or Aquaphor, and switch to a plain unscented hand lotion until the tattoo is fully healed.

DO NOT’S


Don’t re-bandage or cover your tattoo (your skin needs to breathe to heal (properly)
Don’t pick or scratch scabs (they will fall off naturally)
Don’t submerge your tattoo in water (brief exposure to water in the shower is fine)
Don’t use Vaseline, or Neosporin on your tattoo
Don’t expose tattooed area to sunlight for 2 weeks Don’t tan the tattooed area for 8 weeks
Don’t share ointments with anyone
